about me

As someone with a diverse background and a proven track record of success in various fields, I am now embarking on a new chapter in my career by pursuing my passion for web development.
With a keen interest in learning new things and a drive to excel, I am excited to leverage my unique combination of skills and experiences in this dynamic field.
My journey so far has been marked by a wide range of accomplishments. As a CEO of GO HUS, a producer and reseller of tiny/modular houses, I have successfully managed all aspects of the business, from recruiting and leading teams to driving sales and building strong partnerships in the business and private sectors.
Despite the challenges posed by the pandemic, I have overseen a remarkable increase in house sales from 10 per year to an impressive 40-50 per year, showcasing my ability to adapt and thrive in changing markets.
In addition to my entrepreneurial ventures, I have also been the CEO of ICE Wellness, an innovative online company in the sports therapy and wellness industry. Drawing on my fast thinking and execution skills, I have designed and established a successful online business, importing products from China and creating a unique brand in the market. My diverse background extends beyond the business world. With a bachelor's degree in law and previous work experience in law enforcement, including tackling illegal immigration and crimes, I have developed strong analytical and problem-solving skills, as well as the ability to work under pressure. I have also been part of an investigation unit within the Swedish police authorities, where attention to detail and critical thinking were essential. Beyond my professional achievements, I am an individual who loves to travel and explore new cultures with my family. As a homeowner, I have also honed my hands-on skills in building and renovation, showcasing my ability to be both creative and practical. Furthermore, my passion for web development stems from my lifelong interest in computers since I was a kid. I am highly motivated and disciplined, with a competitive spirit fostered through my sports background in track and field, where I was known as one of the most promising and fastest athletes in short distances. As I embark on my journey in web development, I am excited to bring my diverse skill set, entrepreneurial mindset, fast thinking, and proven track record of success to create meaningful and impactful projects. I am confident that my ability to adapt, think critically, and execute efficiently, combined with my passion for learning and exploring new opportunities, make me a strong candidate for a career in web development.
